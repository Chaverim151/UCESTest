What We Do

Always There When You Need Us

Our dedicated volunteers are on call around the clock to provide emergency assistance and peace of mind to the community.

🚗

Roadside Assistance

Flat tires, dead batteries, lockouts — our volunteers respond quickly to keep you safe and on the road.

24/hr Availability

Emergencies don't follow a schedule. Our team is available 24 hours a day.

Community First

We serve all residents of Union and Essex County with kindness, professionalism, and genuine care.
